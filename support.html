Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a game?

Open MindsLinked and tap "Create Session." Choose a card category, then share the session code with a friend. Once they join using "Join Session," the game begins automatically!

My friend can't join my session — what do I do?

Make sure you're both connected to the internet. Double-check the session code (it's case-sensitive). If it still doesn't work, try creating a new session. Sessions expire after a short period of inactivity.

How do push notifications work?

When enabled, you'll receive a notification when a friend invites you to a session or when it's your turn to guess. You can enable or disable notifications in your device's Settings app under MindsLinked.

How do I manage my Premium subscription?

iOS: Go to Settings → your name → Subscriptions → MindsLinked Premium.
Android: Open Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → MindsLinked Premium.
You can change plans or cancel at any time.

How do I delete my account?

Open MindsLinked, go to your Profile, and tap "Delete Account." This will permanently delete your account and all associated data. You can also email us to request deletion.

I found a bug — how do I report it?

Please email us at support@mindslinked.com with a description of what happened, your device model, and OS version. Screenshots help too!
